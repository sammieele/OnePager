The Heart of Liyue

After the death of Guizhong, Rex Lapis brought his people into what is known today as Liyue Harbor. Liyue Harbor is a bountiful harbor located in the Sea of Clouds, Liyue. Come to Liyue Harbor to experience Xiangling's juyuen chili dishes and the trading ports!

The Chasm

The Liyue Qixing has recently declared the Chasm safe for visitors!

Arguably the defining feature of Liyue among its stone forests and lively rivers, the Chasm looks like its namesake. The legends say the Chasm was formed 6000 years ago by a falling star, and during the Archon War, the star rose back to Celestia.

If you chose to adventure below the surface of the Chasm, you might just stumble upon the remains of Khaeniri'ah...

Rex Lapis
